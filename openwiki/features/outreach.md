# Outreach Pipeline

## Features

- Customer segmentation
- Email/SMS campaigns
- Follow-up sequences
- Performance tracking

## Data Model

### campaigns

- `user_id` - Business owner
- `name` - Campaign title
- `channel` - Email/SMS
- `status` - Draft/active/completed

### sequences

- Pre-defined message flows
- Time delays between steps
- Conditional branching

## Integration

- Resend for email
- Twilio for SMS
- Emma AI suggests outreach timing

## Analytics

- Open/click rates
- Conversion tracking
- ROI calculation
