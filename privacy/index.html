Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, phone number, email address, and business details when you sign up for our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our AI receptionist services, communicate with you about your account, and send relevant updates.

SMS Mobile Messaging

Branch Live and its affiliated service businesses send SMS text messages only to people who have given prior express consent, as described below.

How you opt in (double opt-in)

You provide your mobile number when you book an appointment — either by phone with our AI receptionist, Emma, or through the business's online booking form. Right after you book, you receive one confirmation text: "Reply YES to get appointment reminders from [Business]. Reply STOP to cancel." You are added to our SMS list only if you reply YES. We never message a number that has not booked an appointment, and reminders are sent only after you reply YES. There is no automatic opt-in and no pre-checked box.

Message types & frequency

You will only receive appointment-related messages — confirmations, reminders, reschedule notices, and booking follow-ups. We never send marketing or promotional text messages. Frequency is typically 1–4 messages per appointment.

Opt-out

Reply STOP (or CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE, REVOKE, STOPALL) to any message at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive a one-time confirmation and then no further messages. Reply START to resubscribe.

Help

Reply HELP or INFO to any message for assistance, or email hello@branchlive.com. Message and data rates may apply.

Non-sharing of mobile information

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third parties.
