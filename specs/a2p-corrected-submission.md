HOW DO END-USERS CONSENT?:
Customers provide their mobile number when they book an appointment — by phone with our AI receptionist Emma, or through the business's online booking form at branchlive.com/s/{business}/book. Immediately after booking, we send ONE confirmation text: "[Business]: Reply YES to get appointment reminders. Reply STOP to cancel." Only customers who reply YES are opted in and receive reminders. We never message a number that has not booked an appointment. Full details: https://branchlive.com/privacy/#sms-mobile-messaging
