# Branch Live — MRR Projections

> Monthly Recurring Revenue projections at 10, 25, 50, and 100 customers.  
> Base price: $29.95/mo. Upsells: Web $10, GBP $15, Email $20.

---

## Assumptions

### Upsell Attachment Rates (Conservative Estimate)
Based on typical SaaS upsell benchmarks for modular add-ons:

| Add-on | Price | Conservative | Moderate | Optimistic |
|--------|-------|-------------|----------|------------|
| Web Add-on | $10/mo | 15% | 30% | 50% |
| GBP Integration | $15/mo | 10% | 20% | 35% |
| Email Add-on | $20/mo | 20% | 35% | 55% |

**Attachment rate:** % of customers who take that add-on. Customers can take multiple add-ons.

### Scenarios
| Scenario | Web | GBP | Email | Description |
|----------|-----|-----|-------|-------------|
| Conservative | 15% | 10% | 20% | Early-stage, minimal upsell push |
| Moderate | 30% | 20% | 35% | Mature onboarding, upsell prompts in flow |
| Optimistic | 50% | 35% | 55% | Aggressive bundling, discount for multi-add-on |

## Key Takeaways

1. **$29.95 base price is solid** — competitive with other virtual receptionist services, leaves room for upsells
2. **Upsells are the growth lever** — going from conservative to optimistic adds $1,425/mo at 100 customers (a 38% increase)
3. **Churn is the silent killer** — 5% monthly churn means replacing ~40% of customers annually just to stay flat. Customer success investment pays off.
4. **ARPU is stable** — the mix of upsells keeps ARPU consistent regardless of total customer count
5. **Break-even around 30 customers** — achievable with focused local business outreach
6. **At 100 customers with moderate upsell: $51,540 ARR** — this is a viable small business SaaS with room to grow

## MRR Calculation Formula

```
MRR = Σ (base_customers × $29.95) 
    + Σ (web_customers × $10.00)
    + Σ (gbp_customers × $15.00)
    + Σ (email_customers × $20.00)

Where:
  web_customers    = total_customers × web_attachment_rate
  gbp_customers    = total_customers × gbp_attachment_rate
  email_customers  = total_customers × email_attachment_rate

ARPU = MRR ÷ total_customers
ARR = MRR × 12
```
