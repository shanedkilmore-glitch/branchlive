# Branch Live — Pricing FAQ
## The Top 10 Questions Contractors Ask About Price

---

### 1. "How much does it cost?"

**$29.95 per month, flat.** That's it. One plan, one price. No hidden fees, no setup charges, no per-minute billing, no contracts. You get unlimited calls, transcripts, voicemail, appointment booking, SMS reminders, and a custom greeting — all included. Cancel anytime with one click.

---

### 2. "Is there a free trial? Do I need a credit card?"

**Yes — 7 full days, no credit card required.** You sign up with just your name, email, and phone number. We give you your own AI receptionist number instantly. Forward your phone, and Emma starts answering calls within minutes. After 7 days, if you want to keep her, you add your card. If you don't, the service pauses. No charges, no hassle.

---

### 3. "Are there any hidden fees? Setup costs? Per-minute charges?"

**No.** What you see is what you pay:

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Setup fee | $0 |
| Per-minute fee | $0 |
| Per-call fee | $0 |
| SMS charges | $0 (included) |
| Voicemail storage | $0 (unlimited) |
| Cancellation fee | $0 |
| **Monthly total** | **$29.95** |

The only thing you might pay extra for is an optional dedicated local area code number if you need one different from what we assign — that's $5/month. But most contractors use the number we provide, which is included.

---

### 4. "How does this compare to hiring a real receptionist?"

A real part-time receptionist costs **$12–$18/hour**. Even at 20 hours a week, that's **$960–$1,440/month**. A full-time receptionist is **$2,500–$4,000/month** plus payroll taxes, workers' comp, and benefits.

Emma is **$29.95/month**. She doesn't call in sick, doesn't need a lunch break, answers at 3 AM the same as 3 PM, and never forgets to take a message.

| | Real Receptionist | Emma (Branch Live) |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly cost | $2,500–$4,000 | $29.95 |
| Hours | 40/week | 24/7/365 |
| Sick days | Yes | Never |
| Vacation | 2 weeks/yr | 0 |
| Message accuracy | Human error | Transcripts saved |
| Scalable | Hire more people | No extra cost |

---

### 5. "What happens after the 7-day trial?"

On Day 7, you'll see a banner when you log in: *"Your trial is complete. Emma answered [X] calls and booked [Y] jobs for you."* You enter your card for the first month ($29.95), and you're in. Billed monthly from that date. No annual commitment — you can cancel anytime.

If you don't add a card, Emma pauses. Your call history and settings are saved for 30 days. You can reactivate anytime with the same number and all your data intact.

---

### 6. "Can I cancel anytime? Is there a contract?"

**No contract. Cancel anytime.** One click in your dashboard, or email support@branchlive.com. Your service stops at the end of your current billing period — no refunds for partial months, but also no early termination fees, no "you owe us the rest of the year," no phone calls to retention. We make it painless because we'd rather earn your business again than trap you.

---

### 7. "Do you offer contractor or bulk discounts?"

**If you have 3+ business numbers (multiple crews, locations, or partners):** email team@branchlive.com and we'll quote a volume rate — typically **$24.95/month per line** for 3–9 lines, or **$19.95/month** for 10+ lines.

**Trade association partnerships:** If you're part of a local HBA, NARI, or similar trade group, we may have a member rate. Email us and mention your association.

We don't discount individual accounts below $29.95 — the economics don't work at that price point without compromising quality.

---

### 8. "Can I pause my account in the off-season?"

**Yes.** If you're a seasonal contractor (landscaping, snow removal, pool service), you can pause your account during your off months. Your number and all your data are preserved. When you unpause, billing resumes at $29.95/month. No reactivation fee.

Pause length: up to 6 consecutive months. After 6 months, we release the number (but keep your data).

---

### 9. "What payment methods do you accept?"

**Credit/debit cards** via Stripe: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover.

We also support **ACH bank transfers** for contractors who prefer direct debit — email billing@branchlive.com to set it up.

We don't accept checks, money orders, or crypto. Everything is automatic recurring billing — you never have to remember to pay an invoice.

---

### 10. "How do I know it's worth $29.95?"

**Here's the math:** If Emma books you **one extra job you would have missed**, and that job is worth $500 or more, she's paid for herself for 16 months. Most contractors report Emma booking 2–5 jobs a month they would have lost to voicemail or a missed call.

But don't take our word for it. That's what the **7-day free trial with no credit card** is for. Forward your phone. See how many calls Emma answers and what she books. Then decide. If she doesn't earn her keep, you walk away having paid nothing. We structured the trial exactly this way because we're confident you'll see the value.
