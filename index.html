📱 SMS Messaging

Branch Live sends SMS messages to your customers for appointment reminders, confirmations, and follow-ups only. We never send marketing texts or share phone numbers.

How customers opt in

During every phone call, our AI receptionist Emma asks the caller: "I can send you a text reminder before your appointment — would you like that?" The customer verbally agrees or declines. Only customers who say yes receive texts. No automated opt-in, no pre-checked boxes.

Message types

Appointment reminders, booking confirmations, and follow-up messages related to their scheduled service. Example: "Reminder: Your appointment with Riverside Salon is tomorrow at 2pm. Reply STOP to opt out."

Opt out

Every message includes "Reply STOP to unsubscribe." Customers can opt out at any time, and will never receive another text from that business. Standard message and data rates may apply.
