# Branch Live — Mission & 90-Day Goals

> **CEO: Shane Kilmore** | **Date: June 23, 2026** | **Version 1.0**

---

## Mission Statement

**Branch Live gives every tradesman an AI receptionist that never misses a call.**

Contractors lose 30-60% of inbound leads because they can't answer the phone while on the job, under a truck, or buried in a trench. Branch Live replaces the missed call with a conversation. Our AI receptionist — named Emma — answers 24/7, qualifies callers, books estimates, and routes emergencies. She sounds human, learns each contractor's business, and costs $29.95/month — less than a single missed job.

We serve the trades that build America: hardscape, masonry, HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, and landscaping. Our platform is built by a contractor, for contractors — not by a VC-backed team that's never touched a shovel.

**One line: AI that answers the phone so you can run the job.**

## Core Values

1. **Contractor-First** — Every feature starts with "would this help a guy in a trench?"
2. **Sound Human** — Emma must pass for a real receptionist. No robotic IVR energy.
3. **Dead Simple** — 5-minute signup. No training manual. No onboarding call required.
4. **Underpriced, Not Cheap** — $29.95/mo is a steal for 24/7 coverage. Raise it when we've earned it.
5. **Dark & Fast** — Purple/teal dark theme. Portal loads in under 1 second.
