## 7. I'm getting too many spam calls

**Subject:** Re: Reducing spam calls for {business_name}

Hi {customer_name},

Spam calls are frustrating — here's how to shut them down with Branch Live:

**Immediate fixes (available in your plan):**

1. **Enable Call Screening** — Go to **Settings → Call Screening** and toggle it ON. Emma will ask unknown callers a quick verification question ("Press 1 to continue or state your name"). Bots and auto-dialers fail this and get dropped. Takes 30 seconds to enable.

2. **Block specific numbers** — Go to **Call Logs**, find the spam number, click the **⛔ Block** icon. Blocked numbers hear a "number not in service" tone.

3. **Enable Known Caller Bypass** — Under Settings → Call Screening, toggle "Bypass screening for contacts." Import your client list so real customers skip the screening while unknown callers get filtered.

4. **Adjust spam sensitivity** — Settings → Call Screening → Sensitivity. We offer Low / Medium / High. Start at Medium and increase if spam persists.

**Advanced (if spam is severe):**
- Reply with the area codes or number patterns you're seeing, and I can set up **bulk pattern blocking** (e.g., block all calls from a specific exchange)
- If you're on a public-facing number (Google listing, Yelp, etc.), consider using a **separate Branch Live number** for those listings and keeping your main line private

**What Branch Live already does automatically:**
- Known spam databases (STIR/SHAKEN verified) are checked on every incoming call
- Numbers with high spam scores are flagged in your call log
- Robocall patterns (silence detection, DTMF tone patterns) are filtered

Let me know if you'd like me to enable any of these settings on your account — happy to do it for you.
