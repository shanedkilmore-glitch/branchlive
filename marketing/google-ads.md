## NEGATIVE KEYWORDS (Add to all campaigns)

Prevent ad spend on irrelevant searches:

| Negative Keyword | Reason |
|-----------------|--------|
| "jobs" | Job seekers, not business owners |
| "hiring" | Recruiting searches |
| "salary" | Job seekers |
| "free" (standalone) | Tire-kickers looking for everything free |
| "software development" | Tech industry searchers |
| "medical" | Medical answering services |
| "lawyer" / "attorney" | Legal answering services |
| "DIY" | Homeowners doing it themselves |
| "how to become" | Career-changers, not prospects |

## LANDING PAGE REQUIREMENTS

Every ad click should land on a page that:
- Has a headline matching the ad's promise (message match)
- Shows the 3-step "how it works" above the fold
- Includes a clear free trial CTA button (no scrolling required)
- Loads in under 2 seconds on mobile
- Has no navigation bar distractions (keep them on the conversion path)
