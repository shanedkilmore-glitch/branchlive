# Branch Live — SEO Keyword Plan
## Target Audience: Local Contractors (Hardscape, Masonry, HVAC, Plumbing)

---

## 5 PRIMARY KEYWORDS

### Keyword 1: "AI receptionist for contractors"
- **Search Intent:** Commercial — contractors looking for an automated phone answering solution
- **Monthly Volume (est.):** 200–400 (low competition, high intent)
- **Target Page:** Homepage
- **Why It Wins:** Direct match. The searcher knows exactly what they want and is ready to compare options. Very few companies target this phrase today.

### Keyword 2: "answering service for HVAC companies"
- **Search Intent:** Commercial — HVAC owners comparing answering services
- **Monthly Volume (est.):** 500–900 (medium competition)
- **Target Page:** Industry landing page `/hvac`
- **Why It Wins:** High volume, specific trade. The existing competition is human call centers at $200–800/mo. Branch Live at $29.95/mo wins on price immediately once they land.

### Keyword 3: "never miss a call from customers contractor"
- **Search Intent:** Informational/Commercial — contractors searching for the problem, not the solution
- **Monthly Volume (est.):** 100–250 (very low competition, problem-aware buyers)
- **Target Page:** Blog post → homepage
- **Why It Wins:** These searchers know they have a problem (missed calls) but haven't found the solution yet. A blog post titled "How Contractors Stop Missing Job Calls (Without Hiring Anyone)" captures them early.

### Keyword 4: "virtual receptionist for plumbing business"
- **Search Intent:** Commercial — plumbers looking for phone coverage
- **Monthly Volume (est.):** 300–600 (medium competition)
- **Target Page:** Industry landing page `/plumbing`
- **Why It Wins:** "Virtual receptionist" is the phrase these business owners know. They're searching for it. Branch Live positions as the affordable AI alternative.

### Keyword 5: "how to answer business phone while on job site"
- **Search Intent:** Informational — contractors actively frustrated with the problem
- **Monthly Volume (est.):** 150–350 (very low competition)
- **Target Page:** Blog post → homepage
- **Why It Wins:** Long-tail, super specific, zero competition. The searcher is literally describing their pain. A helpful article that leads to a free trial is a direct pipeline.

## SEO EXECUTION PLAN

### Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1–2)

**Technical SEO:**
- [ ] Verify branchlive.com loads under 2 seconds (mobile + desktop)
- [ ] Ensure site is mobile-responsive (90%+ of contractor searches are on phones)
- [ ] Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
- [ ] Set up Google Business Profile for Branch Live (local SEO signal)
- [ ] Add schema markup: `Organization`, `SoftwareApplication`, `FAQ`

**On-Page — Homepage:**
- [ ] Title tag: `AI Receptionist for Contractors | Branch Live — $29.95/mo`
- [ ] Meta description: `Never miss another job call. Branch Live answers your phone, books appointments, and sends you the details — for $29.95/month. 14-day free trial. Built for hardscape, HVAC, plumbing & masonry.`
- [ ] H1: `AI Receptionist That Answers Your Phone While You Work`
- [ ] H2s targeting secondary keywords throughout the page

### Phase 2: Content Creation (Week 2–4)

**Industry Landing Pages (one per trade):**
- [ ] `/hvac` — targeting "answering service for HVAC companies"
- [ ] `/plumbing` — targeting "virtual receptionist for plumbing business"
- [ ] `/hardscape-masonry` — targeting "AI receptionist for masonry contractors"
- [ ] `/construction` — targeting "virtual receptionist for construction companies"

Each landing page structure:
- H1 with the trade-specific keyword
- 3 benefits specific to that trade
- 1 trade-specific testimonial
- Pricing table
- Free trial CTA

**Blog Posts (problem → solution pipeline):**

1. **"How to Answer Business Calls While You're on a Job Site"**
   - Target keyword: "how to answer business phone while on job site"
   - Format: 1,200 words. Lists 5 methods (call forwarding, voicemail, family member, call center, AI receptionist) — positions Branch Live as the clear winner on cost and capability.
   - CTA: "Try Branch Live free for 14 days — no credit card."

2. **"What Missed Calls Are Costing Your Contracting Business"**
   - Target keyword: "never miss a call from customers contractor"
   - Format: 1,500 words. Do the math (missed calls × closing rate × average job value = monthly loss). Shows Branch Live as the fix.
   - CTA: Free trial.

3. **"Human Call Center vs. AI Receptionist: What Contractors Should Know"**
   - Target keyword: "AI receptionist for contractors"
   - Format: 1,500 words. Comparison table: cost, availability, trade knowledge, setup time. Branch Live wins every category.
   - CTA: Free trial.

4. **"How Much Does an Answering Service Cost? (Real Contractor Prices)"**
   - Target keyword: "affordable answering service for small contractors"
   - Format: 1,200 words. Transparent pricing comparison: call centers ($200–800), hiring staff ($2,500+/mo), Branch Live ($29.95). Clear winner.
   - CTA: Free trial.

5. **"5 Signs Your Contracting Business Needs a Receptionist"**
   - Target keyword: "virtual receptionist for plumbing business" (secondary)
   - Format: 800 words. Relatable signs ("You're apologizing for missed calls," "Your spouse is your receptionist"). Positions Branch Live as the fix.
   - CTA: Free trial.

**Link Building:**
- [ ] Guest post on 3 contractor trade blogs (Hardscape Today, Plumbing & Mechanical, Contracting Business)

- [ ] Partner with 3 contractor coaching/consulting businesses for cross-promotion
- [ ] HARO/Connectively — respond to journalist queries about small business tech

**Local SEO:**
- [ ] Create location pages for top 10 metro areas: `/phoenix`, `/charlotte`, `/san-antonio`, `/denver`, `/portland`, `/dallas`, `/atlanta`, `/orlando`, `/philadelphia`, `/houston`
- [ ] Each location page: city name + "AI receptionist for contractors in [City]"

**Social Proof:**
- [ ] Collect 20+ Google reviews from trial users
- [ ] Record 5 video testimonials (60 seconds each) for landing pages
- [ ] Display review count and star rating on homepage

### Phase 4: Monitoring & Iteration (Ongoing)

- [ ] Track keyword rankings weekly (Google Search Console)
- [ ] Monitor click-through rate on primary keywords → adjust meta descriptions if under 3%
- [ ] A/B test homepage headline variants (the 3 options provided in homepage copy)
- [ ] Track conversion rate: visitor → free trial → paid
- [ ] Add new blog posts monthly targeting long-tail trade-specific questions
